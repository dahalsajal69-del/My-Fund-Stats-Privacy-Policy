Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2023

At My Fund Stats, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Data Ownership

All financial data you enter is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. We have no access to the information you input into the app.

Local Storage

We use your device's local storage (such as IndexedDB or LocalStorage) to save your records, theme preferences, and settings. This data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to any external servers.

Data Access

Your data is always accessible directly on your device. Because the app functions locally, you can view and manage your stats without needing a constant internet connection.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your information. You can permanently delete all your data at any time through the "Delete All Data" option located in the Settings menu. Additionally, uninstalling the app will typically remove all locally stored data associated with the application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
